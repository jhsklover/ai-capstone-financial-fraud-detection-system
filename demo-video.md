## Demo Video

The demonstrational video walks through the three workflows featured during this project: Transaction Ingestion, Anomaly Detection, and Case Management. The fourth and final component is the dashboard, or the UI of the system in which financial analysts can interact with to view metrics and trends. Each workflow updates the Airtable base, which in turn, updates the Dashboard view.
